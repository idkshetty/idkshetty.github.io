Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at Mudball Federation. Here's how we collect, use, and protect your data when you support us.

What We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Selected Perk
  • Extra Donation Amount
  • UPI Transaction ID
  • Reason for Donation
  • Timestamp

How We Use It

  • To thank and reward you with your selected perk.
  • To verify your donation via UPI Transaction ID.
  • To reach out in case of issues or clarification.
  • We never sell or share your data. Ever.

Data Protection

All your info is securely stored using Firebase. Only trusted Mudball team members have access.

Consent

Submitting the form gives us permission to store and use your details as described above.
